Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how GitDelivr handles your information.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect personal data unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via the contact form or newsletter). We do not track your browsing history outside of this website.

2. Repository Data

When you generate a link, we process the repository URL to construct the GitDelivr link. We do not store your code or repository contents on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for hosting, analytics, and database services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

4. Cookies

We use local storage to save your theme preference (light/dark mode). We may use anonymous analytics cookies to improve the service.
